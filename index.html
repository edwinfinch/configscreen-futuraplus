Celcius/Fahrenheit

    • Choose your preferred temperature conversion. (Weather preference updates and temperature synchronizes when you save settings)


    Weather Update Interval

    • How often the weather updates.


    Battery Saving Mode

    • Turns off minutely/hourly animations (boot-up animation stays on), and vibrations.


    Date Format

    • Organize the date in different formats


    Seconds

    • Enables seconds beside the minute (Warning: smaller font)


    Bootup Animation

    • Changes the animation that slides elements onto the screen upon booting the watchface.


    Bluetooth Disconnect Warning

    • Enable/disable the text and vibration that appear on bluetooth disconnect.


    Bluetooth Reconnect Warning

    • Enable/disable the text and vibration that appear on bluetooth reconnect.
